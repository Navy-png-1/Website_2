There he was.

Sitting there with his friends, a lunch tray in one hand and a shiny, red can of Coca-Cola in the other. Brown hair. Onyx, beautiful eyes I could drown in for eternity. My cheeks started turning red as I blushed at the sight of him, wearing roses that were reddish on my face like I had run the mile. I forgot how to breathe when my dark, brown eyes met his. I was out of breath as I stared into those circles of darkness that would plunge me in. Love at first sight, people would call it.
